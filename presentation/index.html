Overview

  • Dmitry can share his AlertCloud phone number with you to access his health data from the SHIN-NY API.
  • Upon texting/phoning the AlertCloud number, it will let you know that:
    - He has Type 1 Diabetes
    - He is taking Novolog
    - He has seasonal allergies
  • It will also alert a person by text designated by Dmitry that the AlertNumber number has been called and your number
  • An optional Advanced Directive (i.e. I perfer to be taken to the hospital in a limo)
